Instructions for Banner Images

To replace the banner images, place your own images in this folder with the following names:
- banner-1.jpg
- banner-2.jpg
- banner-3.jpg
- banner-4.jpg
- banner-5.jpg

For optimal display, use images with the following specifications:
- Recommended aspect ratio: 16:9 or similar landscape format
- Minimum resolution: 1200 x 675 pixels
- Optimal file size: Less than 300KB per image for good performance

The images will automatically be displayed in the banner slideshow.
